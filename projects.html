Library Management System

This project is essentially software designed to streamline some of the operations in a library. It helps to ditch the manual processes and keep track of everything digitally, from the vast collection of books and resources to the members borrowing them. Here's a breakdown of what LMS typically offers:

  • Cataloging and record keeping
  • Member Management
  • Inventory Control
